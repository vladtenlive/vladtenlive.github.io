It’s two in the morning and I just finished editing a new YouTube video. An idea popped into my head and boom two hours of recording, two hours of editing and three hours of coloring footage just dissapeared. I happily finished, went to take a smoke break and suddenly I was surprised and asked myself…

  • Why am I doing this?
  • What do I want to achieve with this video?
  • Why do I need to express my opinion on every noise.
  • Is this how I see my life, is this how I see my future?

I don’t know the right answers yet, maybe I’ll never know, but right now I don’t see myself as a youtuber in the future. I’m still holding out hope to leave something more meaningful than a bunch of videos on YouTube.
